    • Easy Pea & Chorizo Risotto

    Prep Time: 10 Minutes

    Cook Time: 35 Minutes

    Total Time: 45 Minutes

    Step 1: Dice the onion and chorizo and add to the pan with olive oil over a low heat.

    Step 2: Once the onions are transclucent and the choriso is crispy, grate in the garlic and fry until fragrant.

    Step 3: Add in the rice and toast for a minute before adding a good glug of Vermouth. Allow the alcohol in this to evaporate off before pouring in half the chicken stock and the peas.

    Step 4: When most of the stock has been absorbed, add half of whats left in. Repeat this until only a little stock is left.

    Step 5: When you add in the last of the stock also grate in your parmesan and season to taste with, salt, pepper, cumin and paprika.

    Step 6: ENJOY!!
